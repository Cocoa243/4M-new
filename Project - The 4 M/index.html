Love,Fun and Sisterhood

Welcome

The “4M” (pronounced like forum /ˈfɔːrəm/) is place to have a discussion about a kind of relationship that is sometimes put aside in favour of other relationships such as romantic, friendly, parent-child and even co-worker relationships. This relationship is the one among sisters and siblings in general. We do not often think about it but sibling relationships are important for individuals throughout their lifetime. Even people wo do not actually have brothers and sisters might have similar strong bonds with other family members and these bounds are necessary.

“Siblings-the definition that comprises love, strife, competition and forever friends.” – Byron Pulsifer

Let's go on a date

What makes a date "a date"? When we think about a date, we often directly imagine a romantic meeting. A date is NOT ONLY aimed at romantic purposes. A date is a social meeting , is spending time with a person or several people you care about. You don't know where or how to spend time with your sisters and/or brothers? Check this out!

Let's talk about it

There are some kind of discussions that you should have or you better have with your siblings. You might feel uncomfortable to bring up some conversations on the table with your parents. You might be willing to share some really intimate issues with your friends but you are not sure how to open up about them. You might have some questions regarding taboo topics but you don’t feel like you have a space to discuss them. Guess what? Your siblings are just next door.

Let's focus on brothers

Brothers matter too! Whether you are a sister to a bigger or younger brother, or you are male and have brothers, don’t miss out on your brotherhood bonds.

Let's travel

Traveling brings us closer together. This works for couples, for parents and children, for friends groups (good ones!) and also for siblings. Be a travel buddy to your sisters (or brothers) and enjoy the world!

Let's chill

Who said that to enjoy time with your siblings you had to do something extra,something exceptional? You might as well spend the night just chilling at home with your siblings doing almost nothing and still living your best life with them. Check out some things you might do on your daily routine.
