CHYL CAFÉ

WHY DO WE LOVE CHYL CAFÉ?
The atmosphere - The Chyl Café at Toison d’Or ,situated at the 1st floor of a retailing building offers a view of the one of Brussels most famous streets : Avenue de la Toison d’Or. The key word to describe the decoration are: ethnic, nature and sustainable. The Moroccan pillows, plants and hanging light bulbs give a warm and atmosphere to the café.
The food- The seasonal cuisine served at Chyl Café is healthy and delicious. Also, they have vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free options. Some people like salads for lunch , other prefer sandwich or something light, some people would rather have a full meal… At Chyl Café you can choose among all this. We definitely recommend this place for lunch !
